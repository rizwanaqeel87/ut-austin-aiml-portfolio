# FoodHub Data Analysis — Customer & Restaurant Insights

## 📌 Business Objective

FoodHub is a food delivery aggregator connecting customers with local restaurants.  
The objective of this project is to analyze order-level data to uncover:

- Customer ordering behavior
- Restaurant performance patterns
- Rating vs delivery time relationships
- Revenue-driving opportunities

The goal is to provide **actionable business insights** to improve customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

---

## 📊 Dataset Overview

- Records: ~1,900 orders  
- Features include:
  - Order cost
  - Delivery time
  - Food ratings
  - Cuisine type
  - Restaurant name
  - Day of week

---

## 🛠 Tools Used

- Python  
- Pandas / NumPy  
- Matplotlib / Seaborn  
- Jupyter Notebook  

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Distribution analysis of order cost & delivery time
- Cuisine-wise and restaurant-wise comparisons
- Rating vs delivery time correlation
- Weekday vs weekend demand trends

---

## 📈 Key Insights

### 1. Delivery Time Directly Impacts Ratings
Orders delivered within ~25 minutes consistently receive higher ratings.

👉 Faster delivery = better customer satisfaction.

---

### 2. Certain Cuisines Dominate Demand
A small number of cuisine categories account for a large percentage of total orders.

👉 Marketing focus on top cuisines yields higher ROI.

---

### 3. Revenue is Highly Skewed
A minority of restaurants generate the majority of platform revenue.

👉 High-performing restaurants should receive priority placement.

---

### 4. Weekends Show Higher Order Volume
Order frequency increases significantly on weekends.

👉 Staffing and logistics should scale accordingly.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### ✅ Optimize Delivery Operations
- Introduce SLA targets for <25 minute delivery
- Incentivize fast-performing restaurants

---

### ✅ Promote Top Revenue Restaurants
- Feature high-performing restaurants on landing pages
- Offer loyalty partnerships

---

### ✅ Cuisine-Based Campaigns
- Run promotions on high-demand cuisines
- Introduce bundles during peak hours

---

### ✅ Weekend Surge Planning
- Increase delivery partners on weekends
- Dynamic pricing for peak periods
